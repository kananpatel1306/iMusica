Who we are?

We are iMusica, a chain of 50 music stores in Auckland, with headquarters in Wellington. Our Annual sales last year were $50 million, and its been growing at about 3 to 5 % per year for the past few years.

We believes that an internet application has great potential, and by using it the right way we want to provide best quality music through CDs and Digital Music. We are interested in expanding our reach beyond Auckland.

- Robison, Vice President.
